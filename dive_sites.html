Manta Point


As the name suggests, Manta Point is known for its frequent sightings of manta rays. Located on the southern coast of Gili Trawangan, this dive site features a cleaning station where mantas gather to have parasites removed by cleaner fish. Divers can often get up close to these majestic creatures and observe their graceful movements in the water.

Shark Point


As one of the most popular dive sites in the Gili Islands, Shark Point is known for its diverse marine life, including reef sharks, turtles, and a variety of colorful fish. The site features a steep slope that drops off into the deep blue, making it an exciting spot for experienced divers. Along with the marine life, divers can also explore the unique rock formations and coral structures.

Halik


Located on the northern coast of Gili Trawangan, Halik is a shallow dive site with a sandy bottom and a variety of coral formations. The site is known for its peaceful and serene atmosphere, making it a great spot for beginners or those who prefer a more relaxed dive. Divers can spot a variety of small marine creatures, such as nudibranchs, sea stars, and crabs, as well as schools of colorful fish.
