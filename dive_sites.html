Boat Departures


Our boat departs three times a day, at 9am, noon, and 3pm, so you can choose the time that works best for you. We offer a safe and exciting diving experience that you'll never forget. Our dive boat is fully equipped with all the necessary safety equipment and our experienced staff is committed to ensuring your safety throughout your entire diving adventure.
